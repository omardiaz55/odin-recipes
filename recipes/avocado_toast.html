Avocado Toast

Description

To make delicious avocado toast, start by toasting two slices of whole-grain bread until they're golden and crisp. While the bread is toasting, halve a ripe avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the creamy flesh into a bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork, and then season it with a pinch of salt, a dash of freshly ground black pepper, and a squeeze of zesty lemon juice. Once the bread is ready, spread the mashed avocado evenly over the slices. For extra flavor and texture, sprinkle red pepper flakes and toasted sesame seeds on top. This simple yet satisfying recipe yields a nutritious and flavorful avocado toast that's perfect for a quick breakfast or snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dash of freshly ground black pepper
  • squeeze of lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes (optional for garnish)
  • Toasted sesame seeds (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Prepare the avocado
  3. Mash and season the avocado
  4. Add lemon juice
  5. Spread the avocado
  6. Garnish
  7. Serve and Enjoy

Remember, you can always customize the recipe by adding additional toppings such as sliced tomatoes, poached eggs, feta cheese, or microgreens, based on your preferences. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious avocado toast!
